Industry-Led, Community-Supported

ODYC is modeled after the Southwest Wyoming Manufacturing Partnership — a coalition built on the principle of being industry-led and community-supported. We transform regional workforce development by creating personalized career roadmaps built around real-world job roles identified by local employers.

Manufacturing & Energy

Electrical technicians, process operators, and maintenance specialists for Wyoming's energy sector.

Healthcare

Medical assistants, pharmacy technicians, and healthcare support roles serving rural communities.

Transportation & Logistics

CDL training, logistics coordination, and supply chain management for regional commerce.
