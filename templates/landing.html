How ODYC Works

A clear path from exploration to employment

1

Join & Explore

Students join ODYC and explore career pathways aligned with Wyoming industries through assessment and interest surveys.

2

Group Mentoring

Students are matched with industry mentors in small groups (5-6 students) for personalized guidance and skill building.

3

Skill Development

Students complete structured curriculum modules, earn industry credentials, and gain hands-on experience through projects.

4

Career Launch

Students transition to internships, apprenticeships, or direct employment with partner companies in Southwest Wyoming.
